Instructions for Using Your Electronic Diary

Welcome to your electronic diary! Here's a quick guide on how to navigate and use the features:

Schools:

In the "Schools" tab, you can view the schools you are associated with. Depending on your role, whether you're a director or a teacher, you will have access to specific information related to your position.

Classes:

Navigate to the "Classes" tab to explore the available classes. Directors will have visibility into all classes, while teachers will only see their assigned classes.

Lessons:

Explore the "Lessons" tab to view and manage your lessons. Directors will have access to all lessons, whereas teachers will only see lessons associated with their classes.
